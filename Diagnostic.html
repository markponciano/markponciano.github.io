The Diagnostic

A fixed-price look at where your admin is costing you money, and what to do about it.

Before anyone builds anything, I find what's actually broken. I don't just interview people, I get into your real data: your reports, exports, bank feeds and transaction history, and cross-check them against each other. That's how I find what interviews miss, the profit report that doesn't match the accounts, the payment that went out twice, the cost nobody's tracking. Read-only access, so I only look, I never touch your live books.

Why start here

  • The report is yours to keep, whether or not we ever work together again.
  • It can pay for itself. My last finance review cross-checked a business's reports and found roughly £80k a year in costs its own reports had missed.
