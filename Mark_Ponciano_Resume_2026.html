  • Reconciliation and controls: built reconciliation scripts that pre-matched about 80% of transactions, so manual review could focus on the high-risk items (WorkCover QLD, icare NSW, patient refunds, HICAPS variances). Kept a daily rhythm, with weekly variance reporting and monthly ledger sign-off.
